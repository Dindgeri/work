Platform Operations

Pluno’s lending platform allows users to borrow assets like stablecoins by locking tokens as collateral in an overcollateralized model. Interest rates are dynamic, based on market supply and demand, and protocol earnings are redistributed to users for a rewarding experience. If collateral value drops, partial liquidation may occur to maintain stability. Learn more in Crypto Borrowing 101.

Pluno operates on Ethereum, Mantle, Taiko, and Morph (via adaptiFi) to maximize liquidity. We’ve partnered with AGNI, mETH Protocol, and Coin98 for campaigns like Methamorphosis and Morph Zoo, and launched Fan Card NFTs on the Viction network. See our AMA Recap for details.

Checking Your Balance

To check your PLUNO balance from the Community Allocation Event (CAE) or Liquidity Bootstrapping Pool (LBP):

  1. Visit ethereum.minterest.com.
  2. Connect the wallet used during CAE/LBP events.
  3. Navigate to the Dashboard and scroll to “Pluno Supporters” to view your balance.

If your balance isn’t displayed, try these steps:

  • Ensure you’re on the Ethereum network.
  • Verify the correct MetaMask account is connected.
  • Clear your browser cache and reconnect.
  • Try a different browser or device.
  • If MetaMask isn’t listed, follow this wallet setup guide.

For CAE/LBP balance calculations, use the CAE Balance Calculator or LBP Calculator. For dashboard access, use impersonator.xyz with WalletConnect.

Withdrawing Assets

To withdraw assets:

  1. Repay any borrow position in full.
  2. Ensure sufficient MNT for transaction fees.
  3. Turn off the collateral toggle.
  4. Select the asset, click “Withdraw,” choose the amount, and confirm.

Compromised Wallets

PLUNO tokens are distributed to CAE/LBP wallets and cannot be reassigned. If your wallet is compromised, ensure borrow positions are repaid and follow withdrawal steps. Contact nextlevel@minterest.com for support.
